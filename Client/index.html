Connect-Map: Connecting Scattered Thoughts, Documents, Photos, Tasks, and More..

Clicking "Connect To Drive" will update any field having a google drive url with the documents at that google drive url.

 Connect To Drive

Clicking "Connect To Gmail" will update any field preceded with the keyword: "gmail-label:" by pulling all of your gmail communications having that keyword.

 Connect To Gmail

clicking "Connect To Asana" will update any field having asana links with the task associated with the project.

 Connect To Asana

clicking "Connect To Trello" will update any field wih a trello link with the title name of the various notes in that project or sub-project.

 Connect To Trello

clicking "Post To Twitter" will post the content of any field whose first words is "twitter:" to your twitter account.

 Post To Twitter

clicking "Post To Facebook" will post the content of any field whose first words is "facebook:" to your facebook account.

 Post to Facebook
